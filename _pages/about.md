I received my B.S. in mathematics from [Fujian Normal University](http://www.fjnu.edu.cn/) in 2016, and M.S. in computer science from [Xiamen University](https://www.xmu.edu.cn/) in 2019. Currently, I am a first year PhD student with the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering at [Shenzhen University](https://www.szu.edu.cn/), under supervision of [Prof. Zhong Ming](http://www.scholat.com/zming) and [Prof. Weike Pan](http://csse.szu.edu.cn/staff/panwk/index.html).

# Research Interest
My reaserch focuses on considering the ranking problem from a perspective beyond accuracy, mainly include solving various biases in ranking, and providing an explainable reason for model design. The application domain of my research includes Recommender System and Advertising System. I appreciate simple and effective models, and I want to do work that can receive interest from the industry. Current research topics include:
* Counterfactual Learning for RecSys
* Generative Model/AE for RecSys
* Modeling users with social theories in RecSys
